Xunjian Yin
(Xunjian is pronounced as Shoon-Jen)

Hi! I am an incoming CS PhD student at Duke University, supervised by Prof. Shuyan Zhou.

Previously, I received my master's and bachelor's degree from Peking University under the supervision of Prof. Xiaojun Wan. I worked as an intern at Microsoft and Shanghai AI Lab. I also worked at UCSB NLP Group, advised by Prof. William Yang Wang.

Research: I aim to make models more generalizable across real-world tasks. I have focused on evaluating and analyzing the generalization capabilities of models, exploring their knowledge boundaries, and designing methods for knowledge editing, self-alignment, and self-referential agents to improve model generalizability and performance.

I believe in using methods that are simple, generalizable, and graceful.

Feel free to reach out to me if you'd like to discuss research or explore potential collaboration!
