Xunjian Yin
(Xunjian is pronounced as Shwuhn-Jyen)

I am a third-year Master student at the Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology, Peking University, supervised by Prof. Xiaojun Wan. Preivously, I received my bachelor's degree from Peking University in 2022. I worked as an intern at Microsoft. Currently, I am also a visiting student at the UCSB NLP Group, advised by Prof. William Yang Wang.

Research: My research focuses on evaluating, understanding and enhancing Large Language Models and Language Agents, particularly in knowledge and reasoning. I explored benchmarking LLM knowledge boundaries, temporal knowledge editing, unsupervised alignment methods, and self-referential agents.

I am seeking 2025 Fall CS Ph.D. opportunities. Feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research or want to discuss with me.
